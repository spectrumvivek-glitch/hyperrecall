HyperRecall — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 18, 2026
App: HyperRecall (package: com.recallify.app)
Developer: Suyash / HyperRecall Team

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how HyperRecall ("we", "our", "the app") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our mobile application. By installing and using HyperRecall, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account information: Email address and display name when you sign in with Google or email.
  • Study content: Categories, notes, and images you create within the app.
  • Subscription information: Purchase status processed through RevenueCat and Google Play Billing (we never receive your payment card details).

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data: Streak counts, revision history, and study analytics (stored locally on your device and optionally synced to your private cloud account).
  • Device information: Device model, operating system version, and app version for crash diagnostics.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not access your phone number, call logs, SMS messages, or contacts.
  • We do not access your camera, microphone, or precise location.
  • We do not collect your phone state (READ_PHONE_STATE is explicitly blocked in our app manifest).

3. Permissions Used by the App

  • Internet & Network State: Required for cloud sync, Google sign-in, and subscription verification.
  • Notifications: Used to send you spaced-repetition reminders.
  • Vibrate: Provides feedback when reminders fire.
  • Receive Boot Completed & Schedule Exact Alarm: Allow scheduled study reminders to fire on time, even after device restart.
  • Read Media Images: Used only when you choose to attach an image to a note. Images are stored locally and synced only to your own private cloud account.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide core app functionality (creating notes, scheduling reviews, tracking progress).
  • To sync your data securely across your devices when you sign in.
  • To process and verify subscription purchases.
  • To send local notifications based on your study schedule.
  • To diagnose and fix crashes or technical issues.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Local storage: Your notes and study data are stored on your device using encrypted local storage (AsyncStorage).
  • Cloud sync: When you sign in, data is synced to Google Firebase (Firestore) under your own account. Only you can access this data.
  • Authentication: Handled securely by Google Firebase Authentication.
  • Subscriptions: Handled by RevenueCat and Google Play Billing.

We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS) for all data transmitted between your device and our servers.

6. Third-Party Services

HyperRecall uses the following third-party services. Please review their privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase (authentication, cloud database) — Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In — Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat (subscription management) — Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing (in-app purchases) — Privacy Policy
  • Expo (app delivery framework) — Privacy Policy

7. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is shared only with the third-party services listed above, strictly to deliver the functionality you have requested.

8. Children's Privacy

HyperRecall is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us so we can delete it.

9. Your Rights

  • Access: View all your data within the app at any time.
  • Deletion: Delete individual notes, categories, or your entire account from within the app's settings.
  • Export: Export your study data from the app's settings.
  • Withdraw consent: Sign out and uninstall the app at any time.

To request deletion of all cloud-stored data tied to your account, email us at the address below.

10. Data Retention

  • Local data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app.
  • Cloud-synced data remains in your Firebase account until you delete it or close your account.
  • Subscription records are retained as required by Google Play and RevenueCat.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Effective Date" at the top will reflect the latest version. Continued use of the app after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.
